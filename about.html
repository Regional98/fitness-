Common Exercises

The following are common exercises that we encourage our clients to do as part of their daily exercise routine.

Burpee
Burpees are a great, full-body exercise to increase your strength and endurance. Begin standing, drop into a squat, kick your feet back, and then jump up from a squatted position.
Plank
Planks build your core strength. To perform a plank, get in a push-up position and rest your forearms on the floor. Hold the position as long as you can.
Mountain Climber
Mountain climbers are a good cardio exercise. Start in a push-up position, bring one knee to your chest, then switch quickly as though you are climbing a mountain.
